Step-by-Step instructions:

1. Strat the Game
▪️The game begins with all cards face down.
▪️there are 12 cards total (6 pairs).

▪️Click on any two cards to flip them face up.
▪️Try to remember their positions and images.
3. Check for a match
▪️if the cards match, they stay face up.
▪️if they dont match, they flip back.
4. Win the Game
▪️Match all 6 pairs to win!
▪️The game ends once all pairs are found.
▪️if you run out of time you will Lose!
